UniMorph

Temporary Home for the Uni(versal)Morph(ology) Project

Welcome to UniMorph.

The UniMorph project provides morphological resources for many of the world's languages. We are in the process of migrating to our new domain at www.unimorph.org

Downloads

Preliminary extracted Wiktionary morphological tables can be downloaded here. Arabic, Armenian, Czech, Finnish, Georgian, German, Greenlandic, Hungarian, Navajo, Russian, Spanish, and Turkish tables have been through a pass of human vetting, and should be of higher quality than the remaining languages.

The vetted data was used as the basis for the SIGMORPHON2016 shared task

The data is formatted according to the UniMorph feature schema. A description of the schema's construction and contents can be found here.
